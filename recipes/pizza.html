Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons dun-dried tomata pesto
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons mince garlic
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup Alfredo Sauce
  • 2 cups chopped cooked chicken breast meat
  • 1 (12inch) pre-baked pizza crust
  • 1 medium tomato, sliced
  • 1 (4 ounce) package feta cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F(190 degrees C)
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the butter, olive olive , garlic, pesto, basil, oregano, Parmesan cheese, and Alfredo sauce. Arrange the chicken on top of the pizza crust. Pour the Alfredo sauce mixture evenly over the chicken. Top with tomato and feta cheese.
  3. Bake for 10-15 minutes in the preheated oven, until the crust is lightly browned and toppings are toasted. Cut into wedges to serve.
